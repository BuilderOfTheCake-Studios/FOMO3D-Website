What is FOMO3D?

FOMO3D uses the Binance Smart Chain to run a closed ecosystem and reward holders with reflections. By holding FOMO3D, investors gain access to exclusive access to groups, events, and on-chain applications. As a foundational principle, holding the token for longer means that investors are rewarded with more FOMO3D as per the tokenomics. Represented by the hourglass and the symbol ⴵ, FOMO3D reflects how value flows like grains through time — once gone, never returning.
